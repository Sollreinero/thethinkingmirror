Human Perspectives on Artificial Intelligence

What will AI do to us?

For thousands of years, we used tools to change the world. Now our tools are changing us.

Artificial intelligence is no longer somewhere in the future. It's already changing how we think, learn, create and connect with one another.

The challenge now is understanding how AI affects our choices, our relationships and our ideas about what it means to be human.

Who’s flying this plane? Are we pilots or passengers?

The Thinking Mirror explores the human side of artificial intelligence— and the decisions we are making right now.
